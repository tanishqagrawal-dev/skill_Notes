About SKiL MATRIX NOTES

SKiL MATRIX NOTES is a smart academic platform designed to help students learn better, faster, and more effectively.

Our mission is to provide:

  • High-quality, structured notes
  • AI-powered learning tools
  • Smart study planners
  • Real-time analytics and insights

We believe learning should be intelligent, accessible, and student-centric.

SkillMatrix Notes is built using modern technologies such as Firebase, AI models, and cloud-based analytics to deliver a professional and reliable learning experience.

Made for students. Powered by intelligence.
