Privacy Policy for SKiL MATRiX Notes

Effective Date: February 1, 2026

At SKiL MATRiX Notes, we are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe, secure experience for all visitors and users of our website, regardless of where you reside.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: When you sign up or log in, we collect your name, email address, and academic details (such as college, branch, year) to personalize your experience.
  • Usage Data: We track your interactions on the site — such as pages viewed, notes downloaded, and search queries — to improve features and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, tracking pixels, and similar technologies to collect usage information and support analytics and advertising services such as Google Analytics and Google AdSense. These technologies help us monitor site performance and provide personalized recommendations.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Service providers such as Firebase and analytics partners
  • Advertising partners (only aggregated data)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

5. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services such as:

  • Firebase Authentication and Firestore

  • AI APIs for advanced tools

These partners may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as needed to provide services, comply with legal requirements, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

7. Security

We implement reasonable security safeguards to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security due to the inherent risks of technology.

8. User Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal information by contacting us at skilmatrix3@gmail.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised “Effective Date.”

Contact us if you have any questions or concerns regarding your privacy: skilmatrix3@gmail.com
